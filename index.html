Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need to replace my current CRM?+
No. PrimalSales.ai connects on top of your existing CRM — HubSpot, GoHighLevel, or Salesforce. Your reps keep working the same way. We just make every call and every deal dramatically more visible and actionable.
How long does onboarding take?+
Most teams are fully set up within 48 hours. The self-serve onboarding wizard walks you through connecting your CRM, configuring your sales methodology, and setting up your team. Our priority support customers are typically live on day one.
Does it work with our existing sales process?+
Yes. PrimalSales.ai is methodology-agnostic. Whether you run challenger sale, MEDDIC, SPIN, or your own custom framework — the AI adapts to your process, not the other way around. During setup, you configure your exact methodology and the platform scores against it.
How is this different from Gong or Chorus?+
Gong tells you what happened after the fact. PrimalSales.ai changes what happens next. We're not analytics — we're an active training and pipeline intelligence system. Most of our customers use both: Gong for recording, PrimalSales.ai for coaching, training, and deal action.
Can I add or remove seats mid-month?+
Yes. Seats can be added at any time and are prorated to your billing cycle. Removing seats takes effect at the next renewal date. Enterprise plans have custom terms available.

The Methodology

NOT PATTERN MATCHING.
PSYCHOLOGY.

Every other AI sales tool is trained on historical call data and pattern matches what worked in the past. We built PrimalSales.ai on something fundamentally different — a psychological model of how humans actually make decisions.

Phase 0
Source State
Pattern interrupt. Authority positioning. "I'll tell you if we're not a fit."
Phase 1
Present State
Where the prospect is now — mapped across visual, auditory, and kinesthetic channels.
Phase 2
Desired State
Future memory construction. The vision they're moving toward — before you present a solution.
Phase 3
The Delta
Gap quantification. Cost of inaction. Made tangible and real — out loud, in their own words.
Phase 4
Bridge & Close
Your solution as the only logical bridge. Commitment before price. Objections surfaced early, not at the end.
The Core Insight

"Every other sales methodology starts with the problem. Problem-focused selling triggers the neurological threat response — and once that fires, the prospect is no longer in a decision-ready state. We start with what's working, move toward the desired future, and let the gap between those two states do the closing for us."
